About Me

Adimchi is a senior product designer, frontend engineer and mentor currently at Cowrywise, helping over 1.5- million customers build wealth.

His curiosity for design and technology was sparked in 2015 while learning C++ in sophomore year. By final year, he had pivoted into brand design, helping startups define brand voices and create visual assets for growth.

He later transitioned into product design, building expertise in design systems, B2C/B2B/B2B2C fintech, service design, SaaS, and web3 infrastructure . Today, he works at the intersection of design and engineering, shaping thoughtful, human-centered products through research, design thinking, and user insights.

He plays the cello (semi-professionally, lol), enjoys classical music, travelling, trying new food, mobile gaming (CODM) and photography.
